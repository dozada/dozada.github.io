Dozada

Open source POS platform for retailers

Request a demo

We are hardly working to provide the first release on mobile app for small store. Please fill in the request information to be the free newest member and give us more motivation to make it done soon.

  • Sale: Applications support for sale on mobile, tablet and desktop.
  • Inventory: Manage stock at store, including inbound and outbound activities.
  • Loyalty: Manage customer information, level and loyalty points
  • Promotion: Discounts, Voucher codes and promotion rules
  • Timesheet: Check in/check out and manage timesheet at store
  • Task management: Control the activities and cooperations at stores
  • Report: Providing the insight at store

Features

Dozada has a mission to provide all features support for store's operations.
