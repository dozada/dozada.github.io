Dozada

Open source POS platform for retailers

Mobile POS

Mobile POS is an open source mobile app built by React Native and PouchDB support to small store can use as a POS.

Main features for first release:

  • Sale: Create and manage orders
  • Product: Manage products on your own phone
  • Customer info: Manage your customer info easily
  • Offline first: Offline first concept to make sure your business don't missing any transations by network.
